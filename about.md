## Origin
I was born and raised in Rohnert Park, California. I received my first computer(HP Pavilion dv6700) from my parents in March of 2008. This was just the start of my fascination with computers and technology. I used it mainly to play games with my brother like Counter-Strike, RollerCoaster Tycoon, and Runescape. After only a month or so I got into hosting my own servers and modding games. This is my earliest memory of seeing code. I slowly moved on from this as my friends and I got into console based gaming and I left my computer behind. That is till college where I moved to Huntington, WV to attend Marshall University. Not knowing what I wanted to do I came in as a Civil Engineering major and realized that it was not for me. The next semester I switched to computer science and never looked back. This was my reintroduction into coding and tech in general and I loved every part of it. I graduated in Spring of 2019 with a B.S. in Computer Science and then commissioned into the U.S. Army as a 2LT in the Cyber Branch.

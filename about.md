## Origin
I was born and raised in Rohnert Park, California. I received my first computer (HP Pavilion dv6700) from my parents in March of 2008. This was just the start of my fascination with computers and technology. I used it mainly to play games with my brother such as Counter-Strike, RollerCoaster Tycoon, and Runescape. After only a month or so I got into hosting my own servers and modding games. This is my earliest memory of seeing/modifying code. This ended up being a phase (Mom was right..). That is till college where I moved to Huntington, WV to attend Marshall University and took my first computer science course. This was my reintroduction into coding and tech in general. I graduated in the Spring of 2019 with a B.S. in Computer Science and also commissioned into the U.S. Army as a 2LT in the Cyber Branch.

## Present-Day
I am currently a developer for the U.S. Army. My personal interest include my home lab, win32 development and vulnerability research.
